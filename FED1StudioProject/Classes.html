Here is where you can browse our course selection.
We have beginner, intermediate, and advanced versions of each course. No matter how knowledgeable you are we have a course for you.

Class Subjects

  • Inter-Personal Skills

    In this class we will teach you how to interact with different species and organizations. This includes how to present your self to the different crime families in the galaxy as well as the different goverments you will find across the many worlds in our galaxy.

  • Languages

    From Wookie to Huttese, we can teach you any language you want to learn, we also have a recommended selection of languages that will be usefull to any one of our students.

  • Droids

    Droids are found in every part of our galaxy. In this class we will teach you about the many kinds of droids, as well as how to maintain and repair any droids you might own or are paid to take care of.

  • Everything Blasters

    We will be covering anything and everything in regards to blasters in this class. Different types and how to identify them as well as their weaknesses. Of course will teach you how to use them and come out on top of every blaster fight you might find yourself in (we won't ask why your in one). Blaster maintence and repair is also covered in this course.

  • Light-Saber Defense

    While its unlikey that you will ever own or come across anyone with a light-saber, it's nice to be prepared. We will teach you the best ways to protect yourself against the many light-saber forms, and how to counter them with out one of your own. Now if you assailant is able to use the force we reccomend you pray.

  • Space Travel

    In this class we will teach you how to travel the stars. From how to fly your own ship to making repairs to keep it flying we have you covered. By the time you finish this class you will be able to navigate the hyper-space lanes like a pro.
